## Full Description (4000 characters max)

TriciGo Driver is the app for transport drivers in Cuba. Receive ride requests, navigate to your passengers, and manage your earnings transparently.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Sign up and complete your driver profile
2. Go online when you want to work
3. Receive nearby ride requests
4. Accept the ride and navigate to the passenger
5. Complete the trip and receive payment
6. Review your earnings and stats

KEY FEATURES

- Receive rides: Requests come directly to your phone. Accept or decline based on your preference.

- Built-in navigation: Optimized maps to reach the passenger and destination. No need for another map app.

- Earnings dashboard: See how much you've earned today, this week, and this month. Full control of your finances.

- Total flexibility: Work when you want, as much as you want. You set your schedule and zone.

- Professional profile: Your photo, name, and rating visible to passengers. Build your reputation.

- Complete history: Detailed log of every ride with route, duration, distance, and earnings.

- Smart notifications: Alerts for new rides near you. Never miss an opportunity.

- Detailed reviews: Check ratings and comments from your passengers.

- Rate passengers: Leave your feedback on each passenger with tags and comments.

WHY DRIVE WITH TRICIGO?

- More rides: Access a growing base of passengers looking for transport.
- Transparent income: Know exactly how much you earn per ride.
- Be your own boss: Work at your own pace. No schedules imposed.
- Community: Join the most modern driver network in Cuba.
- Support: Dedicated support team for drivers.

REQUIREMENTS
- A vehicle in good condition (triciclo, moto, or car)
- Valid driver's license
- Phone with Android 6.0 or higher
- Complete the verification process

AVAILABILITY
Currently operating in Havana, with expansion plans to other Cuban cities.
